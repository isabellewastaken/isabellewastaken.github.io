HOW TO PLAY — PORTALHTML

Movement

W / A / S / DMove
SpaceJump (double-tap = fly toggle)
ShiftFly down
CtrlSprint

Portal Gun

Left ClickFire Blue Portal (entrance/exit)
Right ClickFire Orange Portal (entrance/exit)
QClear both portals

Aim at a flat wall, floor, or ceiling to place a portal. Walk or fall into a portal to teleport through it. Momentum is preserved — build up speed before entering!

Inventory

EOpen inventory
1–9Select hotbar slot
LMB (on block)Break block
RMB (hotbar block)Place block (when not holding portal gun)

When the Portal Gun is in your hotbar slot, left and right click fire portals instead of breaking/placing blocks.

Debug Tab

In the inventory Debug tab you can manually place blue/orange portals at any block face using the special portal placement items.

ABOUT EDUENGINE v1.0

EduEngine v1.0 is a lightweight voxel engine built from Educraft a0.2.0. It provides chunk-based world generation, vertex-coloured block rendering via Three.js, player physics (gravity, AABB collision, jumping, flying), a hotbar/inventory UI, and raycasting for block interaction.

PortalHTML a0.0.2 extends EduEngine with portal mechanics, pushable companion cube entities, a GLaDOS caption system, and a fixed Test Chamber world generator.
