HOW TO PLAY

Driving

W / Up ArrowAccelerate
S / Down ArrowBrake / Reverse
A / Left ArrowSteer left
D / Right ArrowSteer right
SpaceDrift (hold while turning)
ESCPause

Track

The track is procedurally generated and infinite — it curves, banks and varies forever. The road surface is portal-able: stay on the tarmac! Grass off-track will slow you down.

Drifting

Hold Space while turning to drift. Build up the drift meter (bottom centre) for a speed boost on release. Full meter = white fire boost!

ABOUT EDUENGINE v1.0

EduEngine v1.0 is a lightweight 3D engine originally built from Educraft a0.2.0, using Three.js for rendering. KartHTML adapts it for kart racing with a spline-based procedural track generator, kart physics, and a third-person camera.

KartHTML is a fan project inspired by Mario Kart™ by Nintendo.
