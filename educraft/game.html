Farming

Right-click Dirt with a Hoe to create Farmland. Right-click Farmland with Seeds to plant Wheat. Wheat grows through 3 stages. Break grown wheat to collect Wheat items. Craft Bread in a Crafting Table (placeholder).

Educraft Changelog

a0.2.0

  • Block categories: Blocks, Items, Debug tabs in Inventory (E key)
  • Debug tab contains: Nether Portal, End Portal Frame, End Portal, Bedrock, Water, Lava
  • Items tab: Wood/Stone/Iron/Gold/Diamond/Netherite Sword, Axe, Pickaxe, Shovel, Hoe, Boots, Chestplate, Helmet, Leggings; Seeds, Wheat, Bread
  • Inventory replaces Creative Menu for E key; I key opens old all-blocks Creative Menu
  • Armour equip slots in Inventory; click item to equip, click slot to unequip
  • Armour shown on HUD above hotbar when equipped
  • Shadows added (directional sun light + shadow map)
  • Test mob (G key): rectangle hitbox, random movement and jumps, 10 hearts
  • Added Farmland block
  • Farming: Hoe on Dirt → Farmland; Seeds on Farmland → Wheat (3 growth stages)

a0.1.1

  • Fixed missing script tag breaking all JS; added Oak Sapling, Gravel, Sponge, Glass
  • Main menu restyled (brown bg, grey MC-style buttons); button rows; Advancements greyed

a0.1.0 — Beginning of Alpha

  • 256 build height; flying; obsidian pillar structures; macOS/Linux warning
