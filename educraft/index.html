Educraft is a game about breaking and placing blocks. It is currently under development and is not ready yet.

It can also be about adventuring with friends or watching the sun rise over a blocky ocean. It’s pretty. Brave players battle terrible things in The Nether, which is more scary than pretty.

The game is currently free to play on all browsers, with Windows, macOS, and Linux ports on the way!
